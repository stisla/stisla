Progress

A horizontal bar that fills as work completes.

Example

Pair the bar with a label and a percentage on the row above. The label states the action; the percentage on the trailing edge sits in .text-body-secondary so the eye reads the action first.

Uploading 100% 
<div class="w-100">
  <div class="d-flex justify-content-between mb-1">
    <span class="fw-medium">Uploading</span>
    <span class="text-body-secondary">100%</span>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Upload" aria-valuenow="100" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 100%"></div>
  </div>
</div>

Basic

Wrap the bar in .progress and place a single .progress-bar inside. The wrapper carries role="progressbar" and the aria-valuenow / aria-valuemin / aria-valuemax attributes; the inner element just paints the fill via width.

<div class="d-flex flex-column gap-3 w-100">
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Zero" aria-valuenow="0" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 0%"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Quarter" aria-valuenow="25" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 25%"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Half" aria-valuenow="50" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 50%"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Three quarters" aria-valuenow="75" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 75%"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Full" aria-valuenow="100" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 100%"></div>
  </div>
</div>

Labels

Drop text inside .progress-bar to render the value on the fill. The default bar is too thin to fit a label legibly, so bump the height with --bs-progress-height when you need one inline.

42%
<div class="progress w-100" role="progressbar" aria-label="With label" aria-valuenow="42" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="--bs-progress-height: 1.25rem;">
  <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 42%">42%</div>
</div>

Height

Override --bs-progress-height on a single .progress to size that instance, or set it on a parent to size a group.

<div class="d-flex flex-column gap-3 w-100">
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Tall" aria-valuenow="70" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="--bs-progress-height: 1rem;">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 70%"></div>
  </div>
</div>

Backgrounds

Recolor the fill with any .bg-* utility on .progress-bar. The track stays on --bs-secondary-bg so the contrast holds in both themes.

<div class="d-flex flex-column w-100 gap-3">
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Primary" aria-valuenow="60" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 60%"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Success" aria-valuenow="60" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar bg-success" style="width: 60%"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Danger" aria-valuenow="60" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar bg-danger" style="width: 60%"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Warning" aria-valuenow="60" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar bg-warning" style="width: 60%"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Info" aria-valuenow="60" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100">
    <div class="progress-bar bg-info" style="width: 60%"></div>
  </div>
</div>

Multiple bars

Use .progress-stacked to compose several .progress rows into one track. Each segment carries its own width and ARIA values.

<div class="progress-stacked w-100">
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Synced" aria-valuenow="15" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="width: 15%">
    <div class="progress-bar"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="In progress" aria-valuenow="30" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="width: 30%">
    <div class="progress-bar bg-success"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Queued" aria-valuenow="20" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="width: 20%">
    <div class="progress-bar bg-info"></div>
  </div>
</div>

Striped

Add .progress-bar-striped for a diagonal stripe pattern rendered as a gradient. The stripes follow the bar color.

<div class="d-flex flex-column gap-3 w-100">
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Striped primary" aria-valuenow="60" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="--bs-progress-height: 0.75rem;">
    <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-striped" style="width: 60%"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Striped success" aria-valuenow="60" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="--bs-progress-height: 0.75rem;">
    <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-striped bg-success" style="width: 60%"></div>
  </div>
  <div class="progress" role="progressbar" aria-label="Striped danger" aria-valuenow="60" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="--bs-progress-height: 0.75rem;">
    <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-striped bg-danger" style="width: 60%"></div>
  </div>
</div>

Animated stripes

Chain .progress-bar-animated after .progress-bar-striped to scroll the stripes from right to left. prefers-reduced-motion turns the animation off.

<div class="progress w-100" role="progressbar" aria-label="Animated" aria-valuenow="75" aria-valuemin="0" aria-valuemax="100" style="--bs-progress-height: 0.75rem;">
  <div class="progress-bar progress-bar-striped progress-bar-animated" style="width: 75%"></div>
</div>
