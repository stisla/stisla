Collapse

A JavaScript-driven show/hide toggle for any element.

Basic

Add data-bs-toggle="collapse" to the trigger and point data-bs-target at the .collapse element. Include aria-expanded and aria-controls so the toggle is reachable.

Be patient toward all that is unsolved in your heart and try to love the questions themselves, like locked rooms and like books written in a very foreign tongue. Rainer Maria Rilke 
<div class="w-100">
  <button class="btn btn-outline-secondary mb-3" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#collapseBasic" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="collapseBasic">
    Toggle content
  </button>
  <div class="collapse" id="collapseBasic">
    <div class="card card-body mb-0">
      Be patient toward all that is unsolved in your heart and try to love the questions themselves, like locked rooms and like books written in a very foreign tongue. <cite class="text-body-secondary">Rainer Maria Rilke</cite>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Link trigger

An <a> uses its href as the target, so data-bs-target can be omitted. Functionally identical to a button trigger.

Link with href

Either control will work. The link grabs the target from its href. 
<div class="w-100">
  <p class="mb-2">
    <a class="btn btn-outline-secondary" data-bs-toggle="collapse" href="#collapseLink" role="button" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="collapseLink">
      Link with href
    </a>
  </p>
  <div class="collapse" id="collapseLink">
    <div class="card card-body d-block mb-0">
      Either control will work. The link grabs the target from its <code>href</code>.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Horizontal

Add .collapse-horizontal to slide width instead of height. The inner element needs a fixed width so the panel knows what to animate to.

No man steps in the same river twice. Heraclitus 
<div>
  <p class="mb-2">
    <button class="btn btn-outline-secondary" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#collapseHorizontal" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="collapseHorizontal">
      Toggle width
    </button>
  </p>
  <div style="min-height: 120px;">
    <div class="collapse collapse-horizontal" id="collapseHorizontal">
      <div class="card card-body mb-0" style="width: 300px;">
        No man steps in the same river twice. <cite class="text-body-secondary">Heraclitus</cite>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Multiple targets

Point data-bs-target at a selector to toggle several elements at once, or give two triggers the same target to drive one panel from anywhere.

First

Panel A.
Panel B.
<div class="w-100">
  <p class="mb-2 d-flex gap-2 flex-wrap">
    <a class="btn btn-outline-secondary" data-bs-toggle="collapse" href="#multiCollapseA" role="button" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="multiCollapseA">First</a>
    <button class="btn btn-outline-secondary" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#multiCollapseB" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="multiCollapseB">Second</button>
    <button class="btn btn-outline-secondary" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target=".multi-collapse" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="multiCollapseA multiCollapseB">Both</button>
  </p>
  <div class="row g-2">
    <div class="col">
      <div class="collapse multi-collapse" id="multiCollapseA">
        <div class="card card-body mb-0">Panel A.</div>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="col">
      <div class="collapse multi-collapse" id="multiCollapseB">
        <div class="card card-body mb-0">Panel B.</div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
